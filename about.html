Hi! My name is Connor Pemberton, and I am the creator of Pembcussion. I grew up playing percussion and I quickly learned how chaotic practicing can get. That's why I created Pembcussion - to serve as a practice tool to keep me organized and focused on what my goals are.

Pembcussion was meant to be a platform for private tutoring, or more accurately, practice coaching over video chat. Then it evolved into a hub of practice tools where I can keep all my practice resources and customize them to my liking.
